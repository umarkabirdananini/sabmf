About us

Founded in 2009 by the Governors of the 19 northern states of Nigeria, along with associates and admirers of the late Sir Ahmadu Bello, the Sir Ahmadu Bello Memorial Foundation (SABMF) is a non-governmental organization committed to continuing his legacy of leadership, good governance, and socio-economic development.
We design and implement impactful programs, collaborate with global and local partners, and promote initiatives that uplift the northern region and contribute to national unity and progress.

Vision:

To be a catalyst for peace, unity, and sustainable development in Northern Nigeria.

Mission

To promote the legacy of Sir Ahmadu Bello through programs that foster leadership, education, entrepreneurship, and health for the socio-economic advancement of the region.

Mandate

Conduct research on the life and legacy of Sir Ahmadu Bello.





Design and implement development programs across Northern Nigeria.

Promote values of leadership, tolerance, accountability, and transparency.
